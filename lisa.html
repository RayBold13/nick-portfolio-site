A Multifaceted Solo Debut

Alter Ego introduces listeners to five distinct personas—Roxi, Kiki, Vixi, Sunni, and Speedi—each representing a unique facet of LISA's identity. This conceptual approach allowed LISA to explore various genres while collaborating with artists such as Rosalía, Tyla, Doja Cat, RAYE, Future, and Megan Thee Stallion.

LISA, the global K-pop sensation and member of BLACKPINK, released her highly anticipated debut solo album, Alter Ego, on February 28, 2025. This 15-track album marks a significant evolution in LISA's musical journey, showcasing her versatility and artistic depth.
