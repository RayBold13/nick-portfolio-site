Doja Cat sheds her pop skin and steps fully into hip-hop
Multi-platinum artist Khalid has released his highly anticipated third studio album, Sincere, on August 2, 2024. This 16-track collection marks a significant evolution in Khalid's sound, blending smooth R&B with elements of alternative R&B and trap soul. The album delves into themes of love, self-discovery, and the complexities of adulthood, offering listeners an intimate glimpse into the artist's personal journey.
Headlines from day one
"The album's lead single, "Please Don't Fall In Love With Me," sets the tone with its melancholic melodies and introspective lyrics. Other standout tracks include "Adore U," "Ground," and "Heatstroke," each showcasing Khalid's signature soulful vocals and emotive storytelling. The album features a guest appearance by British singer-songwriter Arlo Parks on the track "Breathe," adding a layer of depth to the project's sonic landscape.
Headlines from day one
Sincere has received critical acclaim for its cohesive production and Khalid's authentic expression. The album debuted at No. 2 on Billboard's Top R&B Albums chart, solidifying Khalid's place as a leading voice in contemporary R&B.
