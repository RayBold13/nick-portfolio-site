Doja Cat sheds her pop skin and steps fully into hip-hop
Atlanta's own Kenny Mason released his latest mixtape, Angel Eyes, on November 20, 2024. Following his 2024 album 9, this 9-track project delves deeper into Mason's signature blend of alternative hip-hop, grunge, and introspective lyricism. With a runtime of approximately 30 minutes, Angel Eyes offers a concise yet impactful exploration of Mason's evolving sound.
Headlines from day one
The mixtape opens with "INTUITION," a haunting track featuring minimalistic production and introspective lyrics that set the tone for the project. Subsequent tracks like "HOW TO SURVIVE" and "BEEN HAVIN" showcase Mason's versatility, blending elements of drum and bass with his unique vocal delivery.
Headlines from day one
"HOODRAT" delivers a high-energy performance with its pulsating bass and rapid-fire delivery, while "TEEN GOHAN" closes the mixtape with a slow-burning buildup and majestic instrumentation, featuring a standout electric guitar section.
